Our Services

Cardiac Diagnostics

We offer comprehensive cardiac diagnostics to accurately assess heart health. Our services include ECG to monitor heart rhythm, Holter Monitoring for continuous recording of cardiac activity over a period, and Stress Testing to evaluate how well your heart works under physical stress. These tests provide a foundation for effective treatment and early detection of heart conditions.

Interventional Cardiology

Our interventional cardiology services provide minimally invasive procedures to treat heart issues. We specialize in procedures such as Angioplasty, which helps open blocked arteries, and Stenting to ensure arteries remain open. These treatments aim to restore healthy blood flow and reduce the risk of further complications, with the expertise of our experienced cardiologists.

Heart Disease Management

We develop personalized care plans for managing chronic heart conditions such as heart failure and arrhythmias. Our team of specialists will work closely with you to create a treatment plan tailored to your unique needs, including medication management, monitoring, and lifestyle adjustments, ensuring that your heart health remains stable and manageable.

Preventative Heart Care

Our preventative heart care program focuses on maintaining heart health through proactive lifestyle changes. We provide guidance on nutrition, exercise planning, and stress management, along with routine check-ups to help prevent the onset of heart disease. By making sustainable changes, you can significantly reduce the risk of cardiovascular issues.

Post-Operative Support

For patients recovering from heart surgery, we offer tailored post-operative support that includes personalized rehabilitation plans. Our team works with you on recovery exercises, monitoring, and dietary guidance to help you regain strength and reduce the risk of future cardiac events.
